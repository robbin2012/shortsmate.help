# FAQ

<details>
<summary>What can I start with?</summary>
Begin with an idea, keywords, a reference link, or a template to jumpstart the workflow.
</details>

<details>
<summary>My visuals look inconsistent. Any tips?</summary>
Keep style keywords and character settings aligned, and validate direction with cheaper versions before polishing.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How are credits charged?</summary>
Credits are consumed by scripts, storyboards, videos, or voice generation—see Credits & Membership for thresholds.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What aspect ratios and lengths are supported?</summary>
9:16 is standard, but 1:1 and 16:9 are available in the product based on platform needs.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What AI models are you using?</summary>
We integrate OpenAI, Gemini, Qwen, Kling, Hailuo, Jimeng, Grok, and more. Switch between them without leaving the platform.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Why is this tool special?</summary>
Built by creators for creators, ShortsMate reflects daily content practices and keeps improving over time.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does generation take?</summary>
Varies by model—keyframes often finish within 30 seconds and most videos render around two minutes.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What's the difference between free and paid users?</summary>
Trial credits cover basics; paid members unlock unlimited models/features plus higher quotas and priority.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How can I request a refund?</summary>
Use the in-product Support link within 24 hours of purchase, include your order number and reason, and our team will coordinate a full refund.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use the output commercially?</summary>
As long as your assets and scripts comply, you can use them commercially; refer to Terms & Policies for full guidance.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I reach support?</summary>
Submit a ticket through the in-product Support link and include project name plus error details for faster handling.
</details>
