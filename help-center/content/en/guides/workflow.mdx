# Workflow

ShortsMate uses a clear four-stage flow to get from idea to publish in just a few steps.

<Steps>

### Enter Idea or Paste Viral Link

- Enter a concept, keywords, or a reference link
- Generate a script and hooks, then choose length and platform

### Character & Asset Preparation

- Pick your preferred style and character direction
- Keep style keywords and character settings consistent

### AI Storyboard, Script & Keyframe Generation

- Build storyboards and keyframes, then refine
- Generate multiple candidates and pick the best

### Perfect, Download & Publish

- Generate video clips and assemble the final cut
- Add music, voiceover, and captions
- Export and publish to your target platform

</Steps>

<Callout>
  It's most efficient to complete a publishable version first, then return to detail optimization.
</Callout>

## Tips

- A common short video ratio is 9:16, with a 15-30s length
- Reuse character and style presets across projects to save time
- Validate direction at low cost, then increase quality and detail
