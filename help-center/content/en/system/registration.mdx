# Registration

We support Google login or email registration. Follow the steps below to create your ShortsMate account.

<Steps>

### Create an account

- Open the website and click Sign Up
- Choose your registration method: use your Google account for one-click login, or register with email
- If registering with email, enter your email address and set a password
- Check your inbox for the verification email and complete verification

### Complete your profile

- Add a display name, language, and time zone
- Create or join a workspace if you collaborate with a team

### Start creating

- Go to Projects and create your first project
- Start from a template or a blank canvas

### Configure creative tools

- Choose a studio to create your storyline series or content themes
- Select AI generator type: use the latest large models to generate images or video clips separately
- Configure generation parameters based on your creative needs to ensure consistent style

</Steps>

<Callout>
  Use a frequently accessed email for password recovery and updates.
</Callout>

## Login and security

- Use the login page to reset your password
- If you notice unusual activity, update your password and contact support

## Common issues

- No verification email: check spam or confirm the address
- Third-party login fails: retry authorization or switch browsers
