# Getting Started

Follow these steps to create your first short video.

<Steps>

### Sign up and log in

- Create your account and verify email
- Enter your personal or team workspace

### Create a project

- Name your project and set a theme
- Choose target platform and aspect ratio

### Generate script and storyboard

- Enter an idea or link to generate a script
- Create storyboards and keyframes to confirm direction

### Generate the video

- Generate clips and assemble the final cut
- Add music, voiceover, and captions

### Export and publish

- Choose quality and export format
- Publish and track performance

</Steps>

  Credits are consumed during script, storyboard, and video generation. See Credits & Membership for details.
</Callout>
